About Our Company

Amongst other activities, Al Fardouss International (AFIL) represents PGS (Petroleum Geoservices ASA) in the region and has been involved in the exploration efforts in Lebanon and Cyprus from early stages in Hydrocarbon Exploration.

AFIL & group of companies has been involved in the services and supplies of oil and gas sectors in the MENA and Mediterranean regions since many years. Its scope of activity includes Lebanon, Tunisia, Cyprus, Jordan, Egypt, Cuba and other countries.

In addition to projects in the service sectors of oil and gas, AFIL is involved in organization of conferences in several countires.
